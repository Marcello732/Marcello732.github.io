What is RWD?
Responsive web design is use CSS and HTML to resize, hide, shrink, enlarge, or move the content to make it look good on any screen.
How encode in RWD?
First set your viewport in your head on website index. Next use @media and orientation: portrait or landscape in CSS. You must to set breakpoint of native resolution. The best that points is 320px, 576px, 768px, 1024px and 1200px. You can of course add your own breakpoint if you want or need this.
What is the best in RWD?
The best is it that you can make your website beautiful and perfect woorking on every screen. This same page can look well on iPhone 4 and 60' TV.
What suprised me in RWD?
When I watched first time video about RWD I was very suprised that it can be done so easy. When I wrote this website that takes me a lot of time, but I think it was worth it.
