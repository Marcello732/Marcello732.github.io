What is RWD?
Responsive web design is use CSS and HTML to resize, hide, shrink, enlarge, or move the content to make it look good on any screen.
How encode in RWD?
First set your viewport in your head on website index. Next use @media and orientation: portrait or landscape. You must to set breakpoint of native resolution.
What is the best in RWD?
The best is it that you can make your website beautiful and perfect woorking on every screen. This same page can look well on iPhone 4 and 60' TV.
